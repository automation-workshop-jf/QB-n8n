Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Application Purpose

This application is an internal business intelligence tool used exclusively by our company to access and analyze our own QuickBooks Online financial data for budgeting, forecasting, and financial planning purposes.

2. Scope of Use

This application:

  • Accesses only our company's own QuickBooks Online data
  • Is not offered as a service to external parties
  • Does not process data for any other companies
  • Is used exclusively by authorized company employees

3. Data Collection

The application accesses the following data from our QuickBooks Online account:

  • Financial statements (Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet)
  • Customer and revenue information
  • Expense and vendor transaction data
  • Account balances and general ledger data

4. Data Storage and Security

All data accessed from QuickBooks Online is:

  • Stored in our private, secure database
  • Protected by industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS for data in transit)
  • Accessible only to authorized employees
  • Not shared with any third parties
  • Subject to our internal data security policies

5. Data Usage

Data is used solely for internal business purposes including:

  • Financial planning and budgeting
  • Business intelligence and reporting
  • Driver-based financial modeling
  • Management decision support

6. Data Retention

Financial data is retained in accordance with standard accounting practices and legal requirements.

7. Third-Party Services

This application does not share data with third-party services except for QuickBooks Online (source of data) and our internal database infrastructure.

8. User Access

Access to this application and its data is restricted to authorized employees only.

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy as needed.

10. Contact Information

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact our support team.
